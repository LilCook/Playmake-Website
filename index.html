Welcome to PlayMake!

PlayMake is a program where you can listen to our custom tracks, and you can guess which track is which in our new gamemode Musicle, which is inspired by the popular game Wordle that was created by the New York Times. We offer a variety of genres and tracks that you can choose from, made with high-tech software to give you the best listening experience possible.Additionally, if you would like to save your information, you can create an account below and login.
